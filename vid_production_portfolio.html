After a long-stretch of left-brained positions in the tech-world, 2018 found me at the start of a fantastic journey as a professional fine-artist, muralist, and YouTube Host of "Courageous Creatives" a channel devoted to the most dynamic, creative, and courageous individuals in the Phoenix metroplex. Don't let those titles fool you. Such a creative endeavor required so much work, learning, study, project-management, and discipline.

Link

I filmed, interviewed, edited, produced, and promoted over 85 videos for the Courageous Creatives YouTube Channel which interviewed some of the most dynamic creatives in Arizona. I worked closely with the leadership team of F.A.B.R.I.C (Fashion and Business Resources Innovation Center) in Tempe to create an educational series on fashion production called “Haute Tips”. I used tools such as TubeBuddy and YouTube Analytics to analyze metrics of success and select content that would be successful. I worked with iMovie and Adobe Premiere to edit videos as well as Hootsuite and Tailwind to promote them on social media.
