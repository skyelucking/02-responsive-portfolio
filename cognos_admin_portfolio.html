My Cognos Analytics experience may be segmented into 3 eras. As an intern, and then full-time Data Warehousing Analyst, for the Kerr-McGee Oil Company, I got a great introduction to IBM Cognos Analytics. Mentored by some of the best data analytics adminstrators and analysts, I learned concepts around multi-dimensional-cube driven analytics, report building, and data-visualizations. As a Cognos Administrator for Hoya Vision Care, I learned about requirements gathering and report creation. Later, as a technical sales rep and then Global Sales Channel manager, for BSP Software, a third-party software that interacted with IBM Cognos Analytics, I learned about software sales presentations and reseller management.


  • Gathered requirements for and implemented BI requests from several departments.
  • I became familiar with the business intelligence suite, Cognos Series 7 (Transformer, Upfront, PowerPlay Enterprise Server, PowerPlay Web, Impromptu Web Reports, NoticeCast, Cognos Web Services, Visualizer).
  • Using Cognos Visualizer, HTML, and ASP I created a custom dashboard for C-Level and department heads for their weekly KPI reviews.
  • Performed routine maintenance, administered security to the Cognos Upfront portal with Access Manager, and scheduled cube updates using PowerPlay Transformer.
  • Created internal websites for the Human Resources on-boarding, Executive Management, and Data Warehousing teams using Active Server Pages, Dreamweaver Ultradev, Macromedia Flash, Adobe Acrobat, and Adobe Photoshop




After my internship at Kerr-McGee, I was hired for a full-time position as a Data Warehousing Analyst I & II. During my time there I:

Developed a corporate training program for all Cognos Impromptu and PowerPlay users. This program included weekly workshops for Impromptu report writers, a technical training manual for PowerPlay users, corresponding .avi files of often used functions (using Camtasia software), and training classes in branch offices.

In the 2 years I was a Cognos (IBM Analytics) Administrator for Hoya Vision Care I did a major upgrade of Cognos software and created a series of sales and finance reports for the company.

To help with the adoption of these upgrades, I created a corporate training program for the sales team to utilize the newly upgraded Cognos system, creating a training and sales dashboard and corresponding set of videos, live training events, and manuals.




During my tenure at BSP Software (now Techdata) as a Technical Sales Representative, I demonstrated the benefits and complexities of our company software (MetaManager) to potential clients, often C-level executives. These were live software demonstrations on our demo environment.

As the Global Channel Manager, I developed and maintained productive relationships at all levels of partner organizations, including executive leadership, sales, event marketing, and pre-sales engineering. I was also flown nationally and internationally to train our resellers on the software and sales presentation associated with it.
